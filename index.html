About This Website

I wanted to do something that demonstrates who I am as a person. That begged the question, who am I? Well, I believe that one of the most defining characteristics about people is the type of music they listen to. So, here is what Apple Music said about my music taste for the year of 2024.

Top Genres

Alternative

The Alternative genre is categorized by unique sounds and themes that emerge within the music. This often differs from mainstream genres, while also using elements similar to them.

Pop

The Pop genre, also known as popular music, is categorized by styles and sounds that appeal to the general public. It is usually catered specifially for a wide audience. Pop music is constantly changing.

Rock

The Rock genre is a style that drew inspiration from blues and country music. It is typically centered on electric guitar with help from bass guitar and drums. It is an easily recognizable genre that is mainly performed in groups.

R&B and Soul

The R&B and Soul genres are styles that go hand in hand. It was created by African Americans and is a blend of gospel, blues, and jazz. The aesthetic is what differentiates this genre from others.

Hip-Hop

The Hip-Hop genre is characterized by rhythmic sounds and typically rapping. While it is sometimes referred to synonomously with the rap genre, rapping may not be the focus of hip-hop music.
